About Event


BLRDEVX TDX19GG would be a two-day learning adventure(13th-14th July 2019), similar to Dreamforce or TrailheadDX, conducted in Namma Bengaluru.

We have exciting Community Speakers, MVPs and Rockstars lined up, taking us through some of the latest feature and products in Salesforce, and also sharing their journey with us.
